# EXIT HACK Content Strategy Proposal (2026-02)

## 1. Overview
**Goal**: Monetization through low-competition long-tail keywords.
**Current State**: 11 articles covering basic service info, nurse/new-grad niche, and failure/trouble topics.
**Strategy**: Avoid "Red Ocean" keywords (e.g., "退職代行 おすすめ", "退職代行 料金") dominated by major affiliates. Focus on **"Attribute-Specific"** (High Conversion) and **"Fear/Curiosity"** (High Traffic) queries.

---

## 2. Keyword Analysis & Categorization for "Low Competition"

### Tier 1: Attribute-Specific (High Conversion)
*Users are ready to quit but unsure if *their specific situation* allows it.*
- **Keywords**: `公務員`, `自衛隊`, `パート`, `派遣`, `契約社員`, `試用期間` (implied with "new grad"), `30代`, `40代`.
- **Why**: High urgency. Answers specific blockers ("Can a civil servant legally use this?").
- **Competition**: Lower than general "resignation agency".

### Tier 2: Fear & Risk Mitigation (High Trust Building)
*Users are scared of consequences. Addressing these fears builds the trust needed for conversion.*
- **Keywords**: `家に来る`, `親バレ` (partially covered), `訴えられる`, `損害賠償`, `懲戒解雇`, `警察沙汰`.
- **Why**: "Safety" is the #1 product of a resignation agency.
- **Competition**: Medium. Needs authoritative content (Consumer Contract Act, Civil Code).

### Tier 3: Curiosity & Reverse Intent (Blue Ocean SEO)
*Capture traffic from "observers" or "undecideds" and funnel them.*
- **Keywords**: `退職代行 使われた` (Used by someone), `使われた会社` (Companies used against), `その後` (Aftermath), `クズ` (covered), `体験談`.
- **Why**: "使われた" (Passive voice) is a unique angle often ignored by affiliates. It attracts:
    1.  Employers/Managers (not target, but good for backlink/authority).
    2.  Anxious employees wanting to know "what happens to my boss/team if I do this?" -> **Perfect conversion funnel.**

## 3. Proposed Article Titles (Priority List)

Following the strategy, here are the top 5 articles to write next.

### Priority 1: The "Government/Public" Niche
**Title**: **【公務員・自衛隊】退職代行は使える？法律の壁と失敗しない業者の選び方【2026年最新】**
- **Keywords**: `退職代行 公務員`, `自衛隊`, `教師`, `違法`, `法律`.
- **Content**: Explain the difference between Civil Service Law and Labor Standards Act. Introduce services that specialize in or can handle public servants (often lawyers are safer here).

### Priority 2: The "House Visit" Fear
**Title**: **退職代行を使うと上司が家に来る？「訪問」を100%防ぐ方法と警察への通報について**
- **Keywords**: `退職代行 家に来る`, `家宅捜索` (misconception), `警察`, `迷惑`, `無視`.
- **Content**: Explicitly state that "House visits are rare but possible." Explain how agencies block this (contact prohibition requests) and what to do if they *do* come (call police).

### Priority 3: The "Reverse" Angle (High Traffic Potential)
**Title**: **部下に退職代行を使われた上司のショックな本音。「ふざけるな」と思う前に知ってほしいこと**
- **Keywords**: `退職代行 使われた`, `使われた ショック`, `逃げ`, `クズ`, `なぜ`.
- **Content**: Write from the perspective of "What happens in the office."
- **Funnel**: "If you are an employee reading this, know that your company will survive. Don't feel guilty." -> Link to "Kuzu" article.

### Priority 4: The "Part-time/Dispatch" Specific
**Title**: **派遣・契約社員が契約途中で退職代行を使うリスクは？損害賠償と「バックレ」の違い**
- **Keywords**: `退職代行 派遣`, `契約社員`, `契約期間内`, `損害賠償`, `バックレ`.
- **Content**: Address the "fixed-term contract" fear (Civil Code 628). Explain "unavoidable reasons" and how agencies negotiate this.

### Priority 5: The "Cost" Reality (Beat the "Free" search)
**Title**: **退職代行に「0円・無料」は存在しない？格安業者のカラクリと後払い・分割の活用術**
- **Keywords**: `退職代行 0円`, `無料`, `安い`, `相場`, `後払い`.
- **Content**: Debunk "Free" (usually means "you do it yourself" or scam). Pivot to "Post-pay" (reliable alternative).
